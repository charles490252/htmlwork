Kenya's Economy

Welcome to the econmic overview of kenya

Overview

Kenya has one of the largest economies in East Africa.It is a lower-middle-income countrywith a diverse economy including agriculture,manufacturing,services and tourism

Main Economic Sectors

Agricultureremains the backbone of the Kenyan economy ,employing over 70%of the rural population .Major exports include tea, coffee and horticultural products.

The sevice sector,particularly telecommunication and finance, is radily growing and contributes significantly to the country's GDP

Challenges

Kenya faces challenges such as unemployment,corruption and infrastructure deficits that affect its economic growth
